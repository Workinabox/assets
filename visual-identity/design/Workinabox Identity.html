01 — Brand & voice

An agentic system,
shipped as software or hardware.

Positioning

Workinabox is a hierarchy of agents that perform vertical work — software, marketing, sales, operations. Humans write the spec. The system executes, reports, and asks when it's blocked.

Voice — 01

Honest, not slick

We don't promise magic. We surface tradeoffs, name what's unclear, and stop when the spec is incomplete. Marketing copy and product copy share the same rules.

Voice — 02

Engineering-credible

Built on Rust, governed by DDD, instrumented end-to-end. The identity reflects that: precise spacing, monospace labels, restrained ornament. Nothing is decorative.

Voice — 03

Industrial, not sci-fi

Think honest tools, shipping crates, well-made machinery. Avoid silicon-valley pastels, glowing brains, glassy gradients, and the rest of the AI-slop vocabulary.

We say
  • Write the spec.
  • The work runs in a box.
  • Agents, hierarchies, tasks.
  • Ships as software, or hardware.
We don't say
  • AI-powered everything.
  • Revolutionary. Disruptive.
  • Replaces your team.
  • The future of work.

The mark, where it gets tiny.

— Ratio · marketing

60 / 30 / 10 — paper, ink, orange. Orange is the smallest share, always.

— Ratio · product UI

Paper, deep, ink, orange — orange reserved for selection & key actions.

— Accessibility

Ink on Paper: 12.4:1 ✓ AAA. Orange on Paper: 4.7:1 ✓ AA Large. Orange on Ink: 4.2:1 ✓ AA Large. Use ink-soft for body, ink for headings.
